About Fourth Official

Like you, we caught the soccer bug and have since enjoyed talking about it. We bring you our takes, reactions and thoughts with a slice of bias (yeah, we're humans).


We may go on an occasional who's the GOAT conversation but we keep most of that off-air.

Even though the podcast and website are served to you out of a rack sitting in some server farm in who knows where, we live, play pickup soccer and record the podcast in Washington, DC.


Sulaimon Lasisi
Co-Founder/CTO

Even though he won't take credit for it, Sulaimon might have invented the nonchalant nutmeg. He is also rumored to have a pending patent for the no-look backheel goal. These days, you may find him at an indoor soccer arena trying to keep up with the pace of the game.


Outside of soccer, Sulaimon is an avid reader, a 90s/'00s hip-hop head and occasionally keeps busy with software projects.


Bori Oludemi
Co-Founder/CEO

Bori is a Milanista with an appreciation for good football. He's a closet Barcelona fan and you might catch him saying Messi is the GOAT.


He plays soccer casually and loves to travel.
